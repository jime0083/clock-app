Critical Alert 申請フォーム入力内容
================================================================================

申請URL: https://developer.apple.com/contact/request/notifications-critical-alerts-entitlement/

【アプリの種類 / App Type】
健康管理 / Healthcare

【バンドルID / Bundle ID】
com.okiroya.app

【アプリについて説明してください / Describe your app】
Wake or Shame (オキロヤ) is a health-focused alarm app that helps users wake up and build morning exercise habits. The app requires users to complete 10 squats to dismiss the alarm. Completed squats are automatically recorded to Apple HealthKit as functional strength training workouts. The app targets users who struggle with waking up on time and want to incorporate exercise into their morning routine.

【クリティカルアラートとして、どのような種類の通知を送信しますか？ / What type of notifications will you send as Critical Alerts?】
Alarm notifications that require immediate user action. When the scheduled wake-up time arrives, the app sends a Critical Alert to ensure the user wakes up, even when the device is in Do Not Disturb mode or Silent mode. The notification prompts the user to open the app and complete squats to dismiss the alarm.

【緊急アラートはどのくらいの頻度で送信されますか？ / How frequently will you send Critical Alerts?】
定期的に / Once per day
（ユーザーが設定したアラーム時刻に1日1回のみ送信）

【この権限が必要な理由と、アプリ内でどのように使用されるかを説明してください。/ Explain why you need this entitlement and how it will be used in your app.】
This app serves as a health improvement tool by combining alarm functionality with exercise requirements. Users set a wake-up time, and the app must reliably alert them regardless of Do Not Disturb or Silent mode settings.

Critical Alert is essential because:
1. The core purpose is ensuring users wake up on time - a standard notification that can be silenced defeats this purpose
2. The app promotes health by requiring physical activity (squats) before dismissing the alarm
3. Missing the wake-up notification could negatively impact users' daily schedules, work, and health routines
4. The alarm is user-initiated and scheduled, not random - users explicitly choose when to receive it

The Critical Alert will only be sent at the exact time the user has set as their alarm. Users have full control over when and if they receive these notifications.
